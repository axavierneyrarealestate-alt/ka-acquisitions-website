Common questions

Answers that set expectations clearly.

What does K&A Acquisitions do?

K&A Acquisitions helps guide investors through the commercial development process, including goal setting, financing direction, land acquisition, design coordination, contractor pricing, construction, leasing, stabilization, and refinance review.

Does K&A provide financing?

No. Financing is handled by financial institutions, lenders, and licensed lending professionals.

Does K&A perform construction work?

No. Construction-related work, permits, utilities, services, and build-out are handled by contractors, construction professionals, utility providers, and licensed trades when required.

Are outcomes guaranteed?

No investment performance, refinance result, occupancy level, income amount, or return is guaranteed.
